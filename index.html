Effective Politics

About us

Why we exist

Our world is changing faster than ever in history. The governance decisions made now will shape the rest of our lives. So we believe we should learn about and help improve these decisions.

How we work

We put kindness and collaboration first. We connect students across all faculties at the University of Waterloo, creating teams of good people who really care about a specific problem. With the mentorship of dozens of professors, each team analyses how to improve a specific policy for their specific problem.

What we do

Our teams work in four-month sprints. They pick a specific policy relevant to their problem, review existing proposals for improvement, and finally present new refinements to a field expert. Throughout this, we support teams with mentorship from professors, inter-team collaboration, and events to make friends with others who care.
