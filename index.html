Effective Politics

About us

Why we exist

Our world is changing faster than ever in history. The governance decisions made now will shape the rest of our lives. So we believe we should learn about and help improve these decisions.

How we work

We put kindness and collaboration first. We connect students across all faculties, creating teams of good people who really care about a problem. With professors' mentorship, each team analyses a policy improvement for their specific problem.

What we do

In 4 months, our teams pick a policy relevant to their problem, review its proposed improvements, and present new refinements to a field expert. Throughout, we support teams with mentorship from professors, inter-team collaboration, and events to make friends with others who care.
